Maggie Recipe

Description

Maggie is a very famous quick noodles. That's it.

Ingredients

  • Maggie Packet
  • Water

Steps

  1. Take a utensil and put 1 cup water for 1 packet maggie.
  2. Turn on the stove and put it on the stove and add maggie masala.
  3. Then add maggie to it and boil it until it looks edible I think.
  4. Now turn off the stove and eat it.
